Types of Loan

We've helped many businesses across the UK reach their potential through funding

Asset Finance

Asset Finance can be a complicated market. We have lenders that specialise in different areas of business whether that be construction, or engineering, or hospitality. We specialise in knowing our market and where to put your enquiry first time to achieve the best results for you. Contact us today with your enquiry and let us find terms for your purchase. 

Bridging Loans

A bridging loan is a short-term funding option used to purchase property from an auction or to fund a small property development. We also have lenders that will lend on a property for business loan purposes. Interest is often rolled up which means that no monthly payments are normally required, and the loan is paid back once the property is sold or remortgaged. Contact us today for more information. 

Business Overdrafts

A business overdraft is, in essence, similar to a personal overdraft. You are given a facility size based on your business turnover, and you can choose to dip in and out of it when capital is required. Manage your payments with interest only options, and only pay for what you use.

Commercial Mortgages

A commercial mortgage is a mortgage for a commercial property acquisition such as an office building, retail shop, restaurant or bar, industrial warehouse, or BTL housing/HMO complex. The commercial mortgage loan is used to acquire, refinance, or develop a commercial property or properties. 

Invoice Finance

Invoice financing is a lending products that allow companies to finance a business’ debtor book in advance. Advances of up to 90% of the invoice are available. Invoices are sold to a factoring company in exchange for an immediate up front payment. Facilities can remain confidential and terms can be flexible which grow as your business grows. Contact us today for more information. 

Unsecured Business Loans

An unsecured business loan is a business loan that doesn’t require any physical security although the Directors of the business may be asked for a personal guarantee. The loan can be used for a number of things including expansion, acquiring a new member of staff or purchasing an asset. Contact us today for more information.
